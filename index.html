1. Understanding Dental Billing

Dental billing involves processing and submitting claims for dental procedures to insurance companies. Unlike medical billing, dental billing requires expertise in procedure codes like CDT (Current Dental Terminology) and often deals with separate insurance providers for dental coverage. Accuracy and compliance are crucial, as errors can lead to claim denials and financial losses.

2. Common Challenges in Dental Billing

  • Frequent Claim Denials: Incorrect coding or incomplete documentation often results in rejected claims.
  • Complex Coding Requirements: Understanding CDT codes and their updates can be overwhelming.
  • Insurance Verification Issues: Verifying patient eligibility and benefits is time-consuming and error-prone.
  • Account Receivables Management: Delayed payments from insurance providers or patients affect cash flow.
